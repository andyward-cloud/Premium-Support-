Premium Support

Minimum Viable Offering

Executive deck (20 minutes)

The problem we’re solving

  • Customer-impacting issues disrupt work
  • Standard support optimizes for fairness
  • Enterprise customers need certainty and speed
When issues occur—bugs, incidents, degradation—the cost is lost productivity, escalation noise, and trust erosion. Standard support is fair and scalable, but not designed for customers who need clear ownership and confidence under disruption.

Why Premium Support exists

Premium Support exists to provide clear ownership and proactive partnership for our most important customers — enabling faster recovery when work is disrupted, deeper value from Cursor, and confidence that their feedback drives improvement.

This anchors everything. Premium Support is a controlled capability: protect productivity + trust during disruption, and help customers get more value even when nothing is broken.

How Premium Support behaves

Speed When Work Is Disrupted

Fast recovery, customer-defined urgency, reduced friction — with clear post-incident learning.

Proactive Partnership, Not Reactive Support

Risk detection, enablement, actionable insights, and a credible feedback loop.

Principles are behavior, not features or contracts. They guide inclusions, exclusions, staffing, and ROI.

What customers actually get (MVO)

  • 24/7/365 coverage for customer-defined P0
  • Priority access to senior support
  • Proactive, customer-specific issue communication
  • Risk monitoring and early outreach
  • Weekly/monthly support insight recaps + quarterly support health reviews
  • Targeted RCA for customer-impacting P0 issues (agreed timeframe)
We intentionally keep this high-level. Full inclusions/exclusions live in the appendix (scope table).

What this is not

  • Not unlimited 24/7 support
  • Not a dedicated engineer or TAM
  • Not guaranteed roadmap influence
  • Not bypassing standard engineering processes
Boundaries are deliberate. They protect scale, quality, and team health—and prevent shadow orgs.

How it’s delivered

  • Pooled senior support team
  • Named Senior TSE per account as escalation owner
  • Follow-the-sun coverage
  • Leadership engaged when impact warrants

Human-led, AI-assisted delivery model

AI helps with detection, routing, and pattern recognition—recovery, communication, and escalation remain human-owned.

Talent bar

  • Judgment over knowledge
  • Calm, credible communication under pressure
  • Escalation judgment & ownership
  • Systems thinking & pattern recognition

Premium eligibility is earned, certified, and re-certified.

Staffing capacity logic

  • 70% direct support and issue handling
  • 20% proactive reporting, insights, and customer engagement
  • 10% personal development and upskilling
Premium Support fails if it becomes 100% reactive. This model protects proactive value and avoids burnout.

Pricing

  • Annual add-on
  • % of ARR
  • Capacity-controlled

Headcount is planned against actual and predicted inbound volume, not customer count.

ROI (how we measure success)

Revenue Protection

  • Renewal outcomes following customer-impacting issues
  • Expansion or contraction trends after issues
  • Commercial/exec escalations tied to issues

Issue Containment & Control

  • Time to customer confidence restored
  • Escalations per customer-impacting issue
  • Variance in issue resolution time
  • Repeat issues tied to same root cause

Customer Productivity & Enablement

  • Reduced downtime during issues
  • Faster unblock for production-impacting problems
  • Increased adoption of key workflows
  • Reduced repeat questions via enablement

Operational Signal Quality

  • Quality/actionability of RCAs
  • Reduction in duplicate or recurring issues
  • Improved prioritization signals to Product & Eng
  • Fewer ad-hoc VIP exceptions

Pilot & next steps

  • Limited pilot cohort
  • 6–9 month evaluation
  • Expand only if data supports it

If it doesn’t work, we stop or change it.
