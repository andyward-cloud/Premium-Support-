Pricing — How Premium Support Is Sold

Premium Support is priced to scale with customer risk and protected by capacity discipline.

How It’s Sold

  • Annual add-on
  • Priced as a % of ARR
  • Minimum contract value to ensure appropriate scale
  • Capacity-controlled enrollment
  • Staffed to inbound volume, not customer count

Why This Model Works

  • Scales naturally with complexity & blast radius
  • Avoids constant repricing conversations
  • Predictable for customers, forecastable for Cursor
  • Prevents overselling and protects delivery quality
“We price Premium Support in a way that scales with customer risk — and we staff it in a way that protects quality and delivery.”
